JR SPORTS & GAMES

Game and Sport are very similar: a gmae is physical or mental activity or contest that
has rule and that student do for pleasue. A sport is a contest or game in which our student do
certain physical activities according to a specific set of rules and compete against
each other. The diffeerence is subtle.
